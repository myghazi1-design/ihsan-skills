# Verified Production Prompts

Curated set of the highest-quality production prompts from the source research. Use these as reference examples when generating new prompts for similar scenes.

**Source attribution:** Cliprise.app (50 prompts), Imagine.art (16 prompts), Atlas Cloud (10 human motion prompts), Atlabs.ai (annotated examples). All captured during April 2026 research.

## Category 1: Product Showcase

### Product Hero — Slow Orbit (Cliprise #1)

```
Slow 360-degree orbit around premium wireless headphones on matte black
pedestal. Soft overhead key light with subtle rim light from behind.
Cool studio environment, shallow depth of field, background falls to
smooth gradient. Brushed metal and leather textures sharp in focus.
Professional product photography aesthetic. No blur, no distortion,
no reflections on pedestal surface.
```

### Cosmetics Beauty Shot (Cliprise #4)

```
Close-up of luxury lipstick tube rotating slowly against cream-colored
backdrop. Soft warm lighting from upper right creating gentle highlight
along the gold casing. Shallow depth of field. Cap lifts off revealing
rich burgundy color. Camera holds static, product rotates on turntable.
Premium beauty commercial aesthetic. No morphing, no color shifts
between frames.
```

## Category 2: Cinematic B-Roll

### Urban Rain Night Atmosphere (Cliprise #10)

```
Medium wide shot of empty city street at night during rainfall. Neon
signs reflect in wet asphalt creating colorful streaks. Camera is static
on tripod. Rain falls at consistent angle. A single taxi passes through
frame from left to right. Teal and orange color grading. Shallow depth
of field softening distant buildings. Moody atmospheric aesthetic. No
crowds, no visible text on signs, no bright daylight.
```

### Coffee Shop Interior Detail (Cliprise #12)

```
Static tripod shot inside a dimly lit specialty coffee bar. Barista's
hands pour steamed milk into espresso creating latte art pattern. Steam
rises, catches backlight from a pendant lamp above. Shallow depth of
field isolating the cup. Warm amber tones. Exposed brick and wood
shelving blurred in background. Intimate cafe atmosphere. No faces
visible, no text, no brand logos.
```

### Candle Flame Macro (Cliprise #17)

```
Macro close-up of a single candle flame against pure black background.
Flame moves gently, casting warm orange-yellow light. Wax pool visible
at base with subtle reflection. Camera holds static, flame is the only
movement. Dark moody atmosphere. Film grain optional. Meditative
contemplative aesthetic. No additional objects, no wind effects, no
flickering light artifacts.
```

## Category 3: Human Motion (from Atlas Cloud research)

### Natural Walking (Atlas Cloud #1)

```
Twilight on a city street. The pavement's still wet from rain. A woman
in a beige trench coat walks through it—nothing special, just walking.
Easy pace. Arms loose at her sides. Each step lands heel-first, then
rolls forward. Behind her, streetlights and neon signs blur across the
wet ground. The camera's low, almost street-level, like someone crouched
with a 35mm. No drama. No action. Just her and the city, moving through
each other.

Negative: sliding feet, moonwalk, floating, stiff legs, robotic movement,
gliding, no foot contact, distorted gait, blurry background
```

**Why it works:** The "heel-to-toe" landing description prevents the
common "gliding walk" artifact. Tracking camera at walking speed improves
stability. [Test this claim yourself.]

### Hand Interaction — Coffee Cup (Atlas Cloud #5)

```
Late afternoon sun. Coming through the window. Warm. Angled. A woman's
hand enters the frame. Just her hand. Fingers close around a ceramic
cup. Thumb rests on top. She lifts it from the wooden saucer. Slow.
Brings it to her mouth. A small sip. Then sets it down. Soft clink when
cup meets saucer. The light catches everything. Her fingers. The tea.
Dust floating. The lens is close. You see the texture of the ceramic.
Her nail catching light. The slight shift in her grip as she lets go.
```

**Why it works:** Hands anchored to an object (ceramic cup) are stable
frame-to-frame. Free-floating hands deform. [High confidence — structural
diffusion model behavior.]

### Facial Expression Change (Atlas Cloud #9)

```
Soft light in the room. Quiet. Even. A man sits with his phone. Looking
down at it. His face is still at first. Just waiting. Neutral. Then
something catches him. His eyebrows lift. Barely at first. Then more.
His eyes widen. Just a little. The way they do when you're not sure
you're seeing right. Then the surprise turns into something else. His
mouth opens slightly. Curves into a smile. Not big. Real. You watch it
move through his face. The muscles shifting. Warmth reaching his eyes.
```

**Why it works:** Expression staged anatomically (eyebrows → eyes →
mouth) prevents sudden facial morphing.

## Category 4: Multi-Shot Storyboard

### Product Ad — Three-Shot Structure (Cliprise #33)

```
Shot 1 (0-5s): Wide shot of sleek laptop on minimalist desk. Slow dolly
forward. Soft studio overhead lighting. Clean white environment.

Shot 2 (5-10s): Close-up of fingers typing on keyboard. Shallow depth
of field. Side-lighting highlights key travel and hand texture.

Shot 3 (10-15s): Medium shot pulling back to reveal full workspace
setup. Monitor, plant, coffee cup. Slow crane rises slightly.

Audio: Subtle ambient electronic music, soft keyboard clicks.

Negative: text on screen, brand logos, distorted hands.
```

### Fitness Brand — Workout Sequence (Atlabs example)

```
Shot 1 (0-3s): Wide shot of empty gym at dawn. Early morning light
through windows. Static camera. Anticipation.

Shot 2 (3-7s): Medium shot of athlete chalking hands. Close framing on
hands and chalk dust. Side-lighting creates dramatic texture.

Shot 3 (7-11s): Low angle wide shot of deadlift execution. Full body
visible. Camera static. Powerful controlled movement.

Shot 4 (11-15s): Close-up of face at top of lift. Determination. Camera
holds. Shallow depth of field with gym blurred behind.

Audio: Deep breath, chalk clap, weight plates loading, exertion sound.

Negative: distorted anatomy, extra limbs, unnatural facial expressions.
```

## Category 5: Dialogue Scenes

### Interrogation Room (fal.ai reference)

```
A sleek modern interrogation room with cold LED lighting. Muted gray
walls, a glass window, security cameras blinking red. Low atmospheric
suspense music hums with deep bass drones.

A detective in a navy suit leans forward slowly. His hands rest calmly
on the table.

[Character A: Lead Detective, controlled serious voice]: "Let's stop
pretending."

Immediately, the suspect shifts in their chair, tense.

[Character B: Prime Suspect, sharp defensive voice]: "I already told
you everything."

The detective slides a folder across the table. Paper scraping sound.

[Lead Detective, calm but threatening tone]: "Then explain why your
fingerprints are here."

The suspect's breathing quickens.

[Prime Suspect, voice trembling]: "That's impossible…"

The detective stands suddenly, chair scraping back. Music tightens with
a rising pulse.
```

### Morning Kitchen Chaos — 4 Characters (fal.ai reference)

```
A busy kitchen in the morning. Cereal pouring. Coffee machine buzzing.
Kids running footsteps. Backpack zippers.

A mother flips toast quickly, stressed.

[Character A: Mom, fast urgent voice]: "Shoes on! We're leaving in five
minutes!"

Immediately, a little girl whines from the hallway.

[Character B: Little Daughter, crying voice]: "I can't find my sweater!"

The older brother groans dramatically.

[Character C: Older Brother, annoyed sarcastic tone]: "Because you never
put it away."

Mom sighs heavily.

[Mom, shouting louder]: "Nobody is fighting before 8 AM!"

The dad walks in calmly sipping coffee.

[Character D: Dad, sleepy amused voice]: "Good morning, team."

Mom turns sharply.

[Mom, exhausted voice]: "Help."
```

## Category 6: Image-to-Video

### Portrait — Subtle Life (Cliprise #42)

```
Add subtle breathing movement and single slow blink. Very gentle head
position adjustment as if listening. Maintain exact facial features and
identity from source. No large movements. Preserve original lighting
and composition. Camera holds static. Natural and restrained.

Negative: morphing features, expression changes, style drift.
```

### Product Photo — Add Camera Orbit (Cliprise #41)

```
Animate with smooth 180-degree camera orbit around the product. Maintain
exact subject position, lighting, and color from source image. Slow
controlled rotation revealing side and rear details. Background stays
consistent. Preserve photographic quality throughout rotation.

Negative: style drift, morphing, color shifts.
```

## Using These Examples

When building a new prompt for a similar scene:

1. Find the closest matching example above
2. Note the structural pattern (scene, character, action, camera, audio, negative)
3. Substitute your specific details while preserving the structure
4. Keep negative prompts short and targeted
5. Version-pin: these examples were tested on Kling 3.0 (April 2026)
