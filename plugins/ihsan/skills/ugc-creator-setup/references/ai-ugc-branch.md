## Authenticity tuning rules

The authenticity premium (see [[ugc-rates-and-monetization]]) is the entire AI-UGC moat. Without it, brands pay commodity rates.

Rules:
- [[nbp]] subjects: relatable, not hyper-polished. Avoid "stunning photorealistic model" prompts. Target: "late-20s woman in casual living room with slight kitchen mess".
- [[cinematography-prompting]]: don't apply "cinematic" tokens. Handheld, shoulder-cam drift, natural window light.
- [[kling-video]]: favor dialogue mode with conversational tone descriptors ("warm nostalgic voice" not "confident authoritative voice").
- Captions: CapCut-native style (not cinematic lower-thirds).

## Pitch-the-brand angle

When the user pitches AI-UGC to a brand, positioning matters:
- Don't lead with "AI-generated" — lead with "retainer of 20 – 30 videos/month at $X".
- Sample pack first. Let quality do the arguing.
- Transparency on request: if the brand asks whether it's AI, confirm it. Don't hide it.
