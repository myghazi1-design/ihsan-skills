# Pitch Templates

Rachel Martinez's pitch template + personalized variants for cold-outreach to brands.

## Source template (verbatim phrasing from the guide)

- **Subject line:** `UGC Collaboration Inquiry`
- **Intro:** 1 – 2 sentences, who you are and what you do
- **Niche:** explicit — "I create UGC in the [niche] space"
- **Fit rationale:** reference the brand's recent campaign by name
- **Attachments:** portfolio link + rates

## Email structure

```
Subject: UGC Collaboration Inquiry

Hi [Brand first name or team],

I'm [your name], a UGC creator focused on [niche 1] and [niche 2].

I've been following [Brand]'s recent [campaign / launch / product] and I think the style of [specific detail you noticed] would pair well with the kind of content I create.

A few of my recent UGC samples:
[Portfolio link]

My current rates:
- Organic video: $[X]
- Paid ad: $[Y]
- 3-month usage rights: +30 – 50%

Happy to film a test piece if that's useful. Let me know if there's a fit.

[Name]
[@handle on TikTok / IG]
```

## Channel preferences (from source)

- **Email > DM.** Emails land in a real inbox; DMs get buried.
- **#UGCWanted** hashtag on TikTok / IG — brand-posted calls for creators.
- **Personalize.** Reference a specific recent campaign — generic templates are caught and ignored.

## Variants by entry signal

### Variant A — Inbound brand reach-out (they found you)
Skip the pitch; go straight to Phase 9 (collaboration type) of the playbook. Ask:
- Deliverable type (organic / ad / whitelisting)?
- Usage window (3 / 6 / 12 months / perpetual)?
- Quantity (single / bundle / retainer)?
- Timeline?

### Variant B — Retainer pitch (you want long-term)
Lead with the bundle. "I can deliver 20 – 25 videos/month for $[retainer]." Retainer conversations are different from one-off — reference [[ugc-rates-and-monetization]] retainer benchmarks.

### Variant C — AI-UGC pitch
See [[ai-ugc-branch]] for positioning rules. Don't lead with "AI-generated." Lead with the bundle + rate. Let samples do the arguing.

## Non-negotiables (Phase 11)

Always attach:
- Portfolio link
- Rates
- Contract pre-draft (after they say yes)
- 2 free revisions policy, $50/each after
- Payment deadline (e.g. 10 days post-delivery)
